**Abstract:**

Is it really about the tools? Or is it really about humans? In this
ignite talk, Ho Ming discusses some of the common barriers he has
consolidated over the last several years talking to developers, IT
ops, managers, and executives who looked to introduce devops into
their organization.

**Speaker:**

Ho Ming Li, Cloud Solution Architect, ActiveState

As a Solution Architect, Ho Ming always has the customer's interest in
mind, always gathering customer requirements, working directly with
customers to understand how ActiveState Stackato can solves customer
problems and integrate seamlessly into their development workflow.
