That's great for developers, but why should the rest of the team care? This talk will discuss several features of Vagrant that will increase consistency and minimize surprises throughout the deployment pipeline. The discussion will be non-technical, focusing on the value for the entire team. ### Podcast Audio-only version is available in the [DevOpsDays Podcast](https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/2015-devopsdays-dc-17-vagrant/id1035163116?i=351026111&mt=2) or as a [direct download](http://traffic.libsyn.com/devopsdays/2015-DevOpsDaysDC-17-Vagrant_for_Developers.mp3). ###
