**Abstract:**

Various ways to make software releases combining Gradle, Jenkins,
Artifactory and Bintray. The audience will be introduced to Gradle
build automation tool, Gradle release plugin, Jenkins Artifactory
plugin release management, and Bintray distribution platform. We'll go
through the steps required to make a successful release, review
differences in approaches and see what are the pros and cons of each
one.
