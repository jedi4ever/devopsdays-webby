--- extension: html filter: - erb - markdown dirty: true proposal: true talk: true selected: false layout: event author: title: "Devops without Ops - why this is destined for failure" --- **Abstract:** A Devops team can increase productivity exponentially – as long as it is done correctly. A good number of development teams are adopting this methodology – but are forgetting a “small” but very important part of the concept – and that is that Operations people have to be part of the equation – without this – the chance of success is close to none. This session will go over a few examples of such occurrences we had in internal projects – and the lessons learned **Speakers:** Maish Saidel-Keesing is a virtualization architect for Cisco Video Technologies Israel and has been working in IT for 13 years, focused on virtualization and cloud for the past 8. Awarded the vExpert award from VMware for the past consecutive 5 years for his contributions to the Virtualization community. Co-author of OpenStack Design Book and the VMware vSphere Design and has participated and a technical reviewer of the several cloud publications. His blog, Technodrone, focuses on Virtualization, Architecture, Cloud and Automation and is one of the top 50 virtualization blogs for the past 4 years. Accredited public speaker.
