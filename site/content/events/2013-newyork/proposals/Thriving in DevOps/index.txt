**Abstract:**

One of the key challenges for DevOps practitioners is how to communicate across silos and foster an interdisciplinary culture.  In this talk, I will introduce three simple practices from Co-Active coaching that can help DevOps adoption in teams and organizations, and take existing DevOps implementations to a new level of effectiveness. You will walk away with powerful and practical tools that you can implement with your teams tomorrow.
