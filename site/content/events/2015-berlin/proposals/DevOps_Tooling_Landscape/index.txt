**Abstract:**

On a regular basis the question is asked what's the best
DevOps tool? Although finding a tool shouldn't be your primary focus,
you'll probably need some. With many people still looking for a *silver
bullet* regarding DevOps, this talk covers the different kind of tooling
that is available for DevOps and focuses on bad practices and good
practices on using these tools.
