**Abstract:**
 
 In this session we will describe key analytics and metrics that can
 be captured and  extracted from various systems. These metrics can
 enhance the agile practices and aid the decision taker with
 actionable information to make informed decisions. Lastly these
 metrics enable collaboration across the various business units and
 departments by providing full transparency of the development
 pipeline and risks.
