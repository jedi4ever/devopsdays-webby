**Abstract:**

OopsOps will explore the good, the bad and ugly of when Ops goes
wrong. It'll cover some case studies, outline some core best practices
and how to best communicate with customers when things go as planned.


**Speaker:**

Benjamin Arent 

Ben Arent spends all days building tools to help developers ship
better software faster at Exceptional.  For the past 2 years he's
focused on making tools that let developers push and rapidity iterate
and improve their products.
