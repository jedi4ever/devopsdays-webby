**Abstract:**

Brent is the key operations engineer within the book The Phoenix Project - by DevOPS Godfarthers Gene Kim, Kevin Behr and George Spafford. Everyone at the conference either has a little Brent within them or knows a Brent in their organisation. From an organisation perspective, how can you identify your Brent and what are some practical strategies on minimising your Brent risk. And if you are Brent, how can you loose your Brent title and unshackle the organisation improve your props.
