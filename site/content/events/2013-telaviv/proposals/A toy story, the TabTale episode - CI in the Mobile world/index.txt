**Abstract:**
 
A use case presenting how CI and automation helped TabTale - a company that creates mobile games and education apps for kids - overcome the challenges and obstacles in the mobile world and deliver a large set of applications to multiple platforms and app stores.
 
**Speaker:**

Yoram Michaeli , [Tikal Knowledge](http://www.tikalk.com)

Yoram is an ALM & DevOps expert at Tikal Knowledge. He has a DevOps background of more than 10 years and a programming background of over 20 years. Yoram previously worked at Pontis, Comverse and Microsoft.
